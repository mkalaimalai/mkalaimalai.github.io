About

Software engineering is more than assembling code — it's the art of translating complex ideas into elegant, working systems. I'm a software engineer and architect based in Bengaluru, passionate about the intersection of artificial intelligence and software craftsmanship.

My work spans the full spectrum: from designing microservices architectures grounded in Domain-Driven Design and Clean Architecture, to exploring how AI agents can transform the software development lifecycle. I believe in building systems that are not just functional, but thoughtfully composed.

When I'm not writing code, I'm writing about it. This site is where I share deep dives into technical concepts, architectural patterns, and the evolving landscape of AI-assisted engineering.

The best architectures emerge from a deep understanding of the problem domain, not from the blind application of patterns.
— A principle I build by
